That's why I've gravitated toward more immersive experiences. Building Your Cultural Calendar

I used to be the person who only went to cultural events when someone dragged me. Now, I actively seek them out. Here's what changed my approach:

  • Start with what you know you like: If you love jazz, find a jazz experience. Don't force yourself into something completely foreign just because it sounds "cultured."
  • Read reviews from people like you: Not everyone's taste matches yours. Find reviewers who seem to have similar preferences and see what they loved.
  • Try one new thing per month: You don't have to overhaul your entire social calendar. Just commit to one cultural experience per month and see what sticks.
  • Go with an open mind: The best experiences are often the ones you're unsure about. That weird-sounding immersive thing? It might be your new favorite.
