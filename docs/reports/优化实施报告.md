## 🔍 SEO Meta标签示例

### 首页（index.html）
```html
<title>AuraArbora - Lifestyle Blog | Fashion, Beauty, Home & More</title>
<meta name="description" content="AuraArbora is a curated lifestyle blog featuring inspiring content on fashion & accessories, health & beauty, home & garden, travel, finance, and food & beverage.">
<meta property="og:title" content="AuraArbora - Lifestyle Blog">
<meta property="og:description" content="Curated lifestyle content featuring fashion, beauty, home, travel, finance, and food inspiration.">
```

### 文章页面（article.html）
```html
<!-- 动态Meta标签，由JavaScript更新 -->
<title id="pageTitle">Article - AuraArbora</title>
<meta name="description" id="articleDescription" content="...">
<meta property="og:title" id="articleOgTitle" content="...">
<meta property="og:image" id="articleOgImage" content="...">
```

## 📝 使用说明

### Favicon文件要求

**需要创建的文件**:
1. `favicon-32x32.png` (32x32像素)
2. `favicon-16x16.png` (16x16像素)
3. `apple-touch-icon.png` (180x180像素)

**设计建议**:
- 使用AuraArbora logo的简化版本
- 保持Morandi配色（#E8D5D3、#C4A29E）
- 确保在小尺寸下清晰可辨

---

### Sitemap更新

**何时更新sitemap.xml**:
- 添加新文章时
- 修改URL结构时
- 添加新页面时

**更新方法**:
编辑`sitemap.xml`，添加新URL并更新`<lastmod>`日期。

---

**如需限制爬取**:
